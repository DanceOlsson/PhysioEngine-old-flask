Welcome to PhysioEngine

Streamlining physiotherapy assessments for professionals and patients alike.

PhysioEngine was born out of a desire to simplify the evaluation process in physiotherapy. Recognizing the challenges faced by physiotherapists in gathering and analyzing patient data, we created a tool that bridges the gap between practitioners and patients. Our platform allows for easy generation of QR codes leading to standardized questionnaires, providing real-time results and enhancing the overall efficiency of assessments.
